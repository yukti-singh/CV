Who We Are..

STUDIO 36 provides dance classes in Delhi. We teach dance forms like Salsa, Bollywood and Contemporary, Hip Hop and B Boying, Jazz etc. STUDIO 36 also offers morning aerobics and yoga classes, wedding choreography, guitar lessons and Zumba.....

Mission

To make Delhiites fitter and better dancers and to bring a smile on people's faces through dance, fitness and music.

Studio Overview

At STUDIO 36, students from all over Delhi learn various dance forms in our centres around Delhi and NCR - the main South Delhi centre is in Amar Colony, Lajpat Nagar, New Delhi. Other centres in Greater Kailash 1, Malviya Nagar, Vasant Vihar, Rajouri Garden and Gurgaon. STUDIO 36 has been featured on 24 x 7 News Channel as one of Delhi's best dance institutes with focus on teaching dance at affordable prices and convenient timings. We've also been featured on various newspapers including Hindustan Times and Times of India.
At STUDIO 36, we teach Salsa, Jazz Contemporary, Bollywood, Hip Hop, B-Boying, Popping and Locking, Power Yoga, Zumba, Aerobics and Belly dancing.

Description

STUDIO 36 continuously looks out for outstanding dancers to use for events and TV programmes. We are actively looking for dancers to form dance groups to participate in various events representing STUDIO 36. We also provide instructors and choreographers to teach at corporate offices, in schools or other places.
The quality of teaching and one-to-one focus helps bring out the best in each student. If you can't join a regular class because of timings, but are passionate about dance, find out about our dance workshops, where we teach dance forms in a tailor made course designed for you at a higher price.
